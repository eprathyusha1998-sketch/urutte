Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Urutte, we collect the following information:

Account Information

When you sign up with Google OAuth, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.

Content

We store the urutus, comments, messages, and other content you create and share on our platform.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our service, including the pages you visit, features you use, and the time you spend on our platform.

Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including your browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our social media platform
  • Authenticate your identity using Google OAuth
  • Enable you to create and share content
  • Connect you with other users and facilitate communication
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Ensure the security of our platform and prevent abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and provide personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with Google OAuth for authentication. When you use this service, you are also subject to Google's privacy policy. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
